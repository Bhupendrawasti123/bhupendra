How This Website Works

Create personalized birthday wishes in just a few simple steps:

  • Enter the recipient's name - This will appear in the birthday greeting
  • Add a photo URL - Provide a link to an image (or leave blank for default)
  • Select a message - Choose from our pre-written messages or use random
  • Generate your card - Click the button to create your personalized birthday wish
  • Save or Share - Download as image or copy the shareable link

When the recipient opens the link, they'll see the beautiful birthday card you created!
